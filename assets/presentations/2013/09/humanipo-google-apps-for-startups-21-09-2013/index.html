Before we begin

Google Apps is a toolset. It's not meant to replace your business process but to enchance it by:


  • Offering easy access online to your data
  • Removing the need for in-house specialists to support your tech
  • and so much that you'll discover ...

With all the API's it's meant to be extenable to fit your unique workflow

What is a Google Apps User

A Apps user is a "normal" Google account but controlled by your domain.


So they will login with Google and will be able to use the services that you enabled in your domain
