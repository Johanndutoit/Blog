Identichip

Idea behind IdentiChip

As the developer and administrator of www.getmeknown.co.za and www.getmehome.co.za it's become abundantly clear that the Microchip / Tag / Animal Identification world is in dire need of a easy to use single point of search.


And that's what IdentiChip tries to solve. By allowing providers to register and open their data through a custom endpoint and have their results included in the search.

Fragmentation

Each Provider has their own database with their own search implementation. This is all good and well if you are the only database of animals in town. But what happens when we have 4 main providers, like at the moment in South-Africa.


Well let's walk you through a typical sitiation that can happen to a VET or someone who picked up and scanned a dog.

The utter confusion.

Let's say you find a lost dog on the streets. You scan this dog and find it's UID. Great! Let's go get it's details.


But wait to which provider do I go ? And if you don't know of all the providers, you'll have no luck.

Do a Google Search

I hear you say ? Doing a search just gives you the list of Providers who matched your search. And your going to try them all ?
That's where IdentiChip steps in. Allowing you to search all those providers (If they are registered) to find details on the Dog and contact the owner.

Be it through our Site

or a App / Website using our API

Single Location for Searching

By providing a single of point for searching it allows for many integration and options.


With the biggest option avaialable to use being that we can organize and notify others of global and regional search statistics and events.


And providing a central list of providers for use by other developers and companies.

Great Idea right ? So I set out to create it.

Edge Cases


During development I stumbled on some edge cases in the searches


  • Duplicates from providers
  • Providers that are down for whatever reason.

So Result of Version 1


On the submission deadline for the first round I had:


  • A Site that could search multiple providers
  • Handles some of the first search edge cases
  • Allowed users to contact the owners.
  • List All Registered and Approved Providers for use by anyone needing the information.
  • Allowed Providers to Register and view their search statistics.

The Basics really and that what I aimed for.

And what do you know ! The App made it to the finals !

Goverment Task Team


I'm currently in talks with a the Task Team.


As mentioned there is a Dire need for such a system and the task team agrees. We may see IdentiChip becoming a requirement !


A couple improvements have already come up from their feedback.


Such as a Flagging option to tell the user searching that the animal was stolen etc.

The Trinity, just imagine


My vision is to have 3 sites.


  • Identichip to search providers
  • Some site to list and view lost animals with a API.
  • Some site to list and view all registered vet with a API.

Why ?


The Goal would be to:

  • Allow searches with no results to automaticly get a chance to register the pet as lost.
  • Notify Task Teams when a Flagged Dog is Searched
  • Allow Charity Sites to Search and List Stolen dogs with a API
  • And so much more!
