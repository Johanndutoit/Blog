The purpose of all this ??

By thinking about each required task in this way

Your are more likely to built systems that can scale up and down as needed

Your goal is to build a system that will allow you to:

  • Power up and down more servers to serve more users and actions that required immediate processing and response

  • Monitor throughput and add/remove servers as needed on actions in the queue

  • Limit processing of larger and often non-operational functions in the backend during off peak hours

Plan and immediately react to resource demands
